Our Purpose

MIT CodeIt empowers middle school girls and builds their self-confidence as problem solvers.
Our team of dedicated MIT students serves as mentors and role models for our girls
as we introduce them to computer science.

Our Values

Curiosity

Those who never stop asking questions never stop learning. We value the art of exploring and seeking answers to our own questions.

Challenge

We learn and grow by pushing our limits as problem solvers. We might not know all the answers right now, but we have the resources to figure them out!

Collaboration

Our most valuable resource is each other. By working together and answering each other's questions, we can build bigger and better things.

Program Details

Who: Girls in grades 6 to 8 with little to no programming experience

When: Saturdays from 11 AM to 3 PM (lunch is provided)

What: During the first four sessions, students will learn basic programming concepts by completing Scratch codelabs. During the last four sessions, girls will work in pairs to create their very own Scratch final projects and present them at the project showcase.

Where: MIT Campus

Spring 2018 Dates

March 3, March 10, March 17, March 31, April 7, April 14, April 21, April 28 (no class on March 24)
